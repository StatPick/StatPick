📖 About StatPick

StatPick combines a comprehensive statistical test selector (originally StatPath) with a clinical machine learning advisor. The statistical decision tree guides you through variable types, assumptions, and study designs to recommend the correct test with code and references. The ML advisor helps clinical scientists choose appropriate algorithms based on sample size, features, and research aim, providing peer‑reviewed citations for AI engineers.

Version: 2.0 (Statistics + ML) – Built for biomedical researchers and clinical AI teams.
